Your logs — optional, but gold

Your game wrote a log file while you played. It tells me exactly what went wrong on your machine, and attaching it is the single most useful thing you can do. It rides along with this form — no separate email:

  1. Click Choose file below.
  2. In the window that opens, click the address bar at the top, paste this line, and press Enter:
    C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Better Together\BetterTogether\Saved\Logs
  3. Pick BetterTogether.log (the newest one). Crashed? You can also add files from ...\Saved\Crashes.
